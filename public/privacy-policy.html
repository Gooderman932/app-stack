Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Budget Meal Platform ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by Poor Dude Holdings LLC. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, zip code, and role (consumer or producer) when you register.
  • Purchase data: subscription status and transaction IDs processed through Google Play Billing / RevenueCat. We do not store full payment card numbers.
  • Usage data: meal plan views, recipe interactions, and shopping list activity to personalize your experience.
  • Producer data: farm name, business address, certifications, and inventory listings if you register as a producer.
  • Location data (zip code only): used to determine Kansas eligibility for Fresh Box deliveries. We do not collect GPS or precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the app, including personalized meal plans and budget tracking.
  • Process subscriptions and physical product orders.
  • Connect Kansas producers with bulk buyers through the Producer Network.
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, delivery updates). We do not send marketing emails without your opt-in.
  • Comply with Kansas food regulatory requirements (KDA MF3138 compliance documentation).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase (database and authentication infrastructure, US-based)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Stripe / Shopify (payment processing for physical goods)
  • Law enforcement when required by law.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@poorduдeholdings.com. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time from the Account screen in the app or by contacting us directly.

7. Security

We use row-level security (RLS) on all database tables, encrypted connections (TLS), and Supabase Auth with bcrypt-hashed passwords. No plaintext passwords are ever stored.

8. Changes

We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and posting a notice in the app.
